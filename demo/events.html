Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need to book or sign up?

Nope! All our events are drop-in. Just turn up at the venue and look for the Bonded Bean table.

Is it really free?

Yes — attending is always free. You just buy your own coffee from the café.

What if I don’t know anyone?

That’s the whole point! Most people come alone. We use conversation cards and small groups to make sure nobody feels left out.

What age group is it for?

Our socials are mainly aimed at 18–35 year olds, but everyone is welcome.

Can I bring a friend?

Absolutely. Bring one, bring five — the more the merrier.

Your next great conversation is waiting.

No pressure, no awkwardness — just good people and better coffee. We’d love to see you at the next one.
