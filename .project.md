Bonded Bean Website Brief
Project Overview
Bonded Bean is a community café initiative focused on reducing loneliness among young people by creating welcoming, low-pressure spaces for real-world connection.
The website should communicate our mission clearly, build credibility, and act as a central hub for engagement, events, and future growth.
Objectives
The website should:
• Clearly explain what Bonded Bean is and why it exists
• Communicate our social impact and mission
• Promote our weekly Bonded Bean Socials
• Encourage people to attend, get involved, or support
• Establish a professional and trustworthy brand presence
Target Audience
• University students (primary audience)
• Young people experiencing loneliness or social anxiety
• Potential partners and sponsors
• Volunteers and supporters
Core Pages Required
1. Home Page
• Clear headline: what Bonded Bean is
• Short mission statement
• Strong visual identity (warm, community-focused)
• Call to action (e.g. “Join Our Socials”)
• Quick overview of impact
2. About Page
• What Bonded Bean is
• The problem (loneliness among young people)
• Our mission and vision
• “Community before commitment” philosophy
3. Events / Socials Page
• Weekly Bonded Bean Social details
• Location: Locos, Brunel University
• Time: Thursdays, 5:30pm – 7:00pm
• Simple explanation of what to expect
• Optional: RSVP or interest form
4. Impact Page
• Key statistics (loneliness data)
• What we are tracking (engagement, confidence, community growth)
• Early validation (first session, attendance, feedback)
5. Get Involved
• Volunteer opportunities
• Partner or collaborate section
• Contact form
Design Direction
The website should feel:
• Warm and welcoming
• Minimal and modern
• Calm, not overwhelming
• Community-focused rather than corporate
Colour Palette
• Cream: #F5EFE6 (background)
• Coffee Brown: #8B5A3C (primary)
• Dark Brown: #6E442C (depth)
• Sage Green: #8FAF8C (accent)
Typography
• Headings: Helvetica or similar clean sans-serif
• Body: Open Sans / clean, readable font
Key Features
• Mobile-first responsive design
• Fast loading speed
• Simple navigation (max 5 pages)
• Clean layout with strong spacing
• Integration-ready (future booking, events, donations)
Optional (if possible):
• Event RSVP form
• Newsletter signup
• Social media integration
Tone & Messaging
The website should feel:
• Human, not corporate
• Clear and simple
• Purpose-driven
• Inclusive and welcoming
Avoid overly technical or formal language.
Inspiration (Direction, not copying)
• Modern café brands
• Community-focused platforms
• Minimalist landing pages
Deliverables
• Fully functional website
• Mobile and desktop responsive
• Editable (so the team can update content easily)
• Basic SEO setup
Future Considerations
• Expansion to a full café model
• Online ordering / loyalty integration
• Community storytelling (blog or testimonials)
• Donation or funding integration
